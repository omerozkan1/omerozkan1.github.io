Privacy Policy

Privacy statement for Prayer, Dua, and Qibla application

App Review Notice

Microphone & Speech Recognition: Meyra uses your device's microphone and Apple's Speech Recognition service strictly for the "Voice Tasbih" feature. We do not record, store, or transmit your voice data to our servers or any third parties. All processing happens locally on your device in real-time.

Location Data: We request access to your location to calculate accurate prayer times, send location-based notifications, and determine the Qibla direction. This data is processed locally and is never tracked or sold.

Overview

Meyra processes certain data to provide features such as prayer times, Qibla, Quran, supplications, nearby mosques, and an AI-powered spiritual assistant. We value user privacy and only use the data necessary for the app to function.

Data Collected

1. Location Data

If you grant location permission, your location data is used to show prayer times based on your district and city and to find the nearest mosques. This data is processed locally on your device.

2. Notification Preferences

Your settings for prayer times, reminder notifications, and other optional alerts are stored on your device.

3. AI Assistant Usage

Questions you write to the Meyra AI Assistant may be sent to the relevant AI service to generate a response for you. This content is processed to provide quality service and analyze technical issues.

4. Subscription and Purchase Information

Premium memberships and in-app purchases are managed by Apple. Meyra may check the subscription status to determine if your premium access is active.

5. Analytics and Bug Reports

Analytical and diagnostic services may be used to improve app performance and identify crash issues.

6. Microphone and Speech Recognition

Meyra uses your device's microphone and Speech Recognition strictly for the "Voice Tasbih" feature. Your voice data is never recorded, sent to servers, or shared with third parties. All processing occurs instantly and only on your device.

How Data is Used?

  • Displaying prayer times and location-specific religious information
  • Operating Qibla and nearby mosque features
  • Scheduling and managing notifications
  • Generating responses with the AI assistant
  • Verifying premium access
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Performing tasbih counting locally with Voice Tasbih
